WHO WE ARE ?

Feed Strays Foundation is India's largest and most-trustworthy online donation platform. We have started this cause for the paws in streets, to give them a better life which they deserve.

WHAT WE DO ?

With your help, we provide food to the stray animals. Everyday our team members together visit stray animals, to feed them and provide necessary medical treatments. Our mission is to give every stray animal a 'pawfect' life.
